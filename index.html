About

I'm a technology leader with over two decades of experience spanning software engineering, data engineering and engineering management. Today I lead global engineering organizations building high-scale messaging and communication platforms in fintech — driving product delivery, platform reliability and AI-driven innovation for systems serving millions of users.

My path went from frontend development in the late 90s, through more than 15 years at Globo — South America's largest media group — working on products like Cartola FC and globoesporte.com, to leading engineering at PicPay, one of Brazil's largest fintechs, through its IPO on Nasdaq. I care about clarity, engineering excellence, and building teams that turn technology into real customer value.
